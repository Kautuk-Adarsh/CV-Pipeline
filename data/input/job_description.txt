POSITION: Junior Associate — Capital Markets
FIRM: LLP Legal Firm
LOCATION: Mumbai, Maharashtra
PRACTICE GROUP: Capital Markets and Securities Law

ABOUT THE FIRM

LLP is a full-service Indian law firm with a dedicated Capital Markets practice advising issuers, investment banks, merchant bankers, and institutional investors on a wide range of domestic and cross-border securities transactions. Our Capital Markets team has advised on over 40 IPOs, 15 QIPs, and numerous rights issues and preferential allotments in the past three years.

ABOUT THE ROLE

We are looking for a Junior Associate to join our Capital Markets practice in Mumbai. The candidate will work directly with senior associates and partners on live transactions from day one. This is not a back-office or research role — the candidate is expected to contribute to drafting, due diligence, and client communication under supervision.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

- Assist in drafting and reviewing offer documents including DRHPs, Red Herring Prospectuses, and Abridged Prospectuses for mainboard and SME IPOs
- Conduct legal due diligence on issuers including review of material contracts, litigation history, regulatory approvals, and corporate governance compliance
- Assist in preparing and reviewing filings with SEBI, NSE, and BSE including DRHP filings, SEBI observation letter responses, and listing applications
- Prepare compliance checklists under ICDR Regulations 2018 and LODR Regulations 2015
- Assist in drafting ancillary transaction documents including underwriting agreements, escrow agreements, and broker agreements
- Review and advise on FEMA compliance for transactions involving foreign investors including FPIs and foreign promoters
- Assist in preferential allotment and QIP transactions including board and shareholder resolutions, placement documents, and post-allotment filings
- Maintain transaction timelines and coordinate with merchant bankers, registrars, and other intermediaries
- Research recent SEBI circulars, amendments, and enforcement actions relevant to ongoing transactions

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS

- LLB or BA LLB (5-year integrated programme) from a recognised Indian law school, or LLB combined with LLM
- Currently pursuing or recently completed the above degree
- No current academic backlogs
- Demonstrated interest in Capital Markets, securities law, or financial regulation through coursework, internships, or publications
- Strong drafting and written communication skills in English

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

- Prior internship experience with a Tier 1 or Tier 2 Capital Markets law firm, SEBI, NSE, BSE, or a leading investment bank
- Hands-on exposure to DRHP drafting, ICDR compliance, LODR compliance, or due diligence in a Capital Markets context
- Familiarity with SEBI regulations including ICDR Regulations 2018, LODR Regulations 2015, SEBI Takeover Code, and FEMA provisions applicable to capital markets
- Academic distinction — merit scholarships, rank in class, or awards
- Participation in corporate law or securities law moots
- Publications or research in capital markets, securities regulation, or financial law

WHAT WE OFFER

- Direct exposure to live Capital Markets transactions from day one
- Mentorship from partners with 15+ years of Capital Markets experience
- Competitive compensation benchmarked to market
- Clear associate progression track
